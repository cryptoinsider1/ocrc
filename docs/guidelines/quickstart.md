# Quick Start Guide

1. **Understand the philosophy**: Read the [manifesto](../manifesto.pdf) to grasp the core ideas.
2. **Get a ROI**: Use the ROI generator (`tools/roi-generator/roi_gen.py`) to create an identifier for your research object.
3. **Package your data**: Follow the RO-Crate profile in `spec/rocrate/` to wrap your data with metadata.
4. **Describe your hypothesis** (optional): Use HDF templates from `templates/hypothesis_template.json.template`.
5. **Share**: Upload your RO-Crate to a public repository (Zenodo, GitHub) and announce it on our community forum.

For detailed examples, see the [examples/](../../examples/) directory.
